Website Design Layout

The website is designed as a single-page photography portfolio. At the top of the page, there is a header section containing the main heading and a brief introductory paragraph.

Below the header is an "About Me" section with a heading (H2), an image of the photographer on one side, and a short biography placed next to the image.

The next section is "My Photos", which contains a list of favorite photographs displayed as links that users can click to view.

A links section includes three links related to photography content.

The "Contact Me" section contains a form with fields for name, email, and message, along with a submit button for users to send messages.

At the bottom of the page, a footer is included to display copyright information.
